2. Use of Service & Features

FXNewsBias provides AI-assisted forex news sentiment analysis for informational and educational purposes only. The Service currently includes:

  • Currency Sentiment Dashboard — AI-generated bullish/bearish scores for 8 major currencies, refreshed via scheduled jobs.
  • Market Pulse — a Risk-On / Risk-Off gauge derived from the relative sentiment of risk vs safe-haven currencies.
  • Sentiment vs Price Divergence Detector — flags pairs where 24h sentiment direction disagrees with 24h price direction by a meaningful margin.
  • Sentiment Streaks — counts consecutive days a currency has stayed in the same sentiment regime.
  • 24h Top Movers — biggest sentiment shifts over the last 24 hours.
  • Live News Feed — forex-relevant headlines aggregated from public RSS sources, with publisher favicons fetched via Google's public favicon service.
  • Economic Calendar, Currency Pages, Pair Pages, Historical Reports.
