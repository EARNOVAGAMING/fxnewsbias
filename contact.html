❓ Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Account Settings → scroll to bottom → click "Delete Account". This is permanent and removes all your posts and data.
Why was my post removed?
Posts are removed for violating community rules. Common reasons: financial advice, signal selling, spam, or offensive content. Check our Terms of Service for full rules.
How do I get the Verified badge?
Verified badges are given to active community members who consistently post quality analysis. There's no application — we review and award them periodically.
Can I advertise on FXNewsBias?
Yes! We accept forex-related advertising. Select "Advertising Inquiry" in the contact form and tell us about your brand and goals.
Is there an API for the sentiment data?
API access is coming soon. If you're interested in early access, select "Data or API Access" in the contact form and tell us about your use case.
