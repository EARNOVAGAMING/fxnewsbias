About FXNewsBias

Institutional-grade forex news sentiment, built by traders for traders. Free.

🎯 Our Mission

We're a small global team of forex traders who believe retail traders deserve institutional-grade fundamental analysis — Free Tier Provides News Sentiments.

News drives currencies. But for years, retail traders have been forced to either read every headline themselves, or rely on technical sentiment indicators that ignore fundamentals entirely. That gap is why so many trades get blindsided by news events that were "obvious" in hindsight.

FXNewsBias closes that gap. We use AI to read the same news institutional traders read, scoring each currency's bias every 3 hours, and presenting it in a format anyone can understand in 5 seconds.

📖 The Story Behind The Name

FXNewsBias = Forex News Bias.

The name describes exactly what we do: we measure the bias (bullish, bearish or neutral lean) of forex markets based on news — not charts, not retail positioning, not technical indicators. Just the fundamental news that actually moves currencies.

Founded in 2025, we're a remote team distributed globally — pooling 15+ years of combined retail trading experience with engineering and AI expertise to build the tool we always wished existed.
