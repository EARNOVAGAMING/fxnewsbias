About FXNewsBias

The free forex sentiment platform built for retail traders who care about fundamentals — not just charts.

🎯 Our Mission

Most retail forex traders lose money — not because they're bad at charts, but because they ignore fundamentals. Central bank decisions, economic data, and geopolitical events drive long-term currency moves. But fundamental analysis is hard, time-consuming, and often locked behind expensive Bloomberg terminals.

FXNewsBias changes that. We aggregate news from 12+ sources, use AI to analyze sentiment, and give you a simple Bullish/Bearish score for every major currency. Free. Fast. Accessible to everyone.

⚙️ How It Works

1

News Aggregation

Every 3 hours, we fetch fresh news from Reuters, BBC, FXStreet, ForexFactory, CNBC, Bloomberg, and 6 more trusted sources.

2

AI Analysis

Claude AI reads every headline and analyzes the impact on each major currency — considering central bank policy, economic data, and geopolitical context.

3

Sentiment Scoring

Each currency gets a 0-100 score: Bullish (60+), Bearish (below 40), Neutral (40-60). With key drivers explaining the why.

4

You Trade Smarter

Use sentiment to confirm your technical setups, avoid trading against fundamentals, and sidestep dangerous news events.

💰 How It's Free

FXNewsBias is supported by display advertising and optional broker affiliate partnerships. We never sell your data. We never push specific brokers. Our content stays independent and our sentiment analysis is never influenced by sponsors.

If you want to support us beyond viewing ads, future Premium plans will offer: ad-free experience, sentiment history, email alerts, and priority data feeds. Core features remain free forever.

👤 Built By A Trader

FXNewsBias is built by a retail trader who got tired of losing money to news events that weren't on the charts. After years of trying expensive subscription services and unreliable signal groups, the simple truth became clear: fundamental sentiment is the missing piece for most retail traders.

We're a small operation, focused on quality over quantity. Every feature is built because traders genuinely need it.
