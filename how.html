How FXNewsBias Works

The complete guide to our forex sentiment system — from news aggregation to your trading decisions.

📊 The Big Picture

Currency markets move on fundamentals — economic data, central bank policy, and geopolitical events. But staying on top of all this is overwhelming. We built a system that does the hard work for you.

Every 3 hours, our pipeline reads news from 12+ sources, uses AI to analyze impact on each currency, and outputs a simple Bullish/Bearish score. That's it.

⚙️ The Pipeline

1
Fetch Latest News
Our system pulls fresh news from 12 trusted RSS feeds every 3 hours. Sources include FXStreet, BBC, CNBC, MarketWatch, DailyFX, Investing.com, Yahoo Finance, ForexLive, Action Forex, FX Empire, Nasdaq and Forex Crunch.
2
AI Reads & Analyzes
All news headlines and summaries get sent to Claude AI. The AI is trained to understand forex fundamentals — central bank language, economic data interpretation, geopolitical impact.
3
Sentiment Scoring
For each major currency, AI outputs a 0-100 sentiment score plus the top 3-4 key drivers explaining why. Pairs are calculated by combining the two currencies' scores.
4
Display On Dashboard
Results are stored and displayed on your dashboard with color-coded cards, key drivers, and historical trends. Refreshes automatically.

🎯 How To Read The Score

Every currency gets a score from 0-100. Here's what the ranges mean:

0-39
Bearish
Negative fundamentals — currency likely to weaken
40-59
Neutral
Mixed signals — wait for clearer setup
60-100
Bullish
Positive fundamentals — currency likely to strengthen

💡 How To Use Sentiment In Trading

✅

Confirm Setups

Found a bullish technical pattern? Check sentiment. If fundamentals also bullish — higher probability trade.

⚠️

Avoid Counter-Trend

Don't fight the fundamentals. If USD sentiment is bearish, going long USD pairs is a low-probability trade.

🎯

Filter Pairs

Trade pairs with biggest sentiment divergence. Bullish currency vs Bearish currency = clearest direction.

📅

Plan Around Events

Use the calendar to avoid trading 30 min before/after high-impact events. Volatility kills accounts.

🔄

Track Sentiment Shifts

When sentiment flips from Bearish to Bullish (or vice versa), that's a major trend signal worth noting.

📊

Combine With Technicals

Sentiment is one tool. Combine with price action, levels, and risk management for best results.
